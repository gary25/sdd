The ones that could be classified as the most beautiful missions don't, for the most part, involve stealing from the Hammerites. How about Mechanist's? The first mission done by AsyluM (who built the majority of The Heist Society), Hidden Agenda, is a beautiful Mechanist mission that rivals any of the missions in the original game.

Belboz' Trickster's Gem Mine series of missions are all good. You'll find some Hammers in some of them, as well as lots of other good things.

The 7th Crystal is a truly beautiful, atmospheric mission set in a huge mansion.

Ominous Bequest, one of the best missions around, is a "don't miss" one.

Lord Alan's Fortress is stunning, as well as fun to play. It's chock full of secrets, if you like searching for them.

The Art of Thievery is another huge, gorgeous mansion. It has a lot of Optional objectives on the Ghost (expert) setting. Don't let them put you off because you can complete it on expert without Ghosting at all, if that's not your thing.

Christine's missions, which include several different series, are all gorgeous to look at as well as fun to play.

Gaetane's missions, L'Arsene, Emilie Victor and The Korrigans (The Goblins) are all excellent, particularly the last two.

The Horns of Canzo is very beautiful, though a tad hard to figure out. There's a walkthrough available at thiefmissions.com if you find it a bit confusing.

A Keeper of the Prophecies is a series that shouldn't be missed. The Cathedral in the third mission is outstanding. Also, of course, Calendra's Legacy, as long as you don't hate the undead, because you have to deal with LOTS of them in the second mission.

Left for Dead is a wonderful mission, set partly in a swamp, that is absolutely loaded with atmosphere. Make sure you have your fog turned on for that one. (Crom's Blade, Schwaa's other big mission, is very good, too. It's set in snowy caves and a partially excavated mansion that was buried in ice. Very creative.)

A Smuggler's Request is an old favourite of mine that gives you the opportunity, on Hard and Expert, to explore a quite extensive town area as well as a guardhouse and a ship. Very fun, though a tad hard at the beginning. If you play this one, use your equipment only when absolutely necessary until you get your main supply back. From then on in, it's not a problem.

I could keep going and going. There are just so many good ones and loads of them are really great but this should give you a few to try out. Just in case you haven't discovered it yet, the best place to download missions, look for walkthroughs, hints, loot lists, etc. is Cheap Thiefmissions. Have fun and post here if you get stuck on any of the missions. We'll do our best to help you out.
